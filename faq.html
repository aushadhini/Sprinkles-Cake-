Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from us

Do you deliver island-wide?
Yes! We deliver across all of Sri Lanka. Delivery fees and times vary depending on your location. Colombo and suburbs typically receive same-day or next-day delivery.
Can I customize my cake?
Absolutely. You can choose your flavor, size, message on the cake, and share design references. Just visit our Customize page and fill in the form — we'll contact you within 24 hours to confirm the details.
How far in advance should I order?
We recommend placing orders at least 3–5 days in advance for custom cakes. For standard cakes from our shop, 1–2 days is usually sufficient. For large orders or wedding cakes, please contact us at least 2 weeks ahead.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, cash on delivery (for Colombo areas), and mobile payment apps. A 50% deposit is required when placing a custom order.
Do you cater to dietary restrictions?
Yes! We offer eggless and dairy-free options on request. Please mention any allergies or dietary needs in the special instructions when placing your order.
How should I store my cake?
Keep your cake refrigerated and consume within 3–4 days. Take it out 30 minutes before serving to bring it to room temperature for the best flavor and texture.
Do you do wedding cakes?
Yes! We specialize in tiered wedding cakes with custom designs. Please reach out via our Contact page or call us directly to discuss your vision and get a quote.

Still have questions? We'd love to help.
