Our Story

Baked with passion, served with love

Sprinkles Cake was born from a simple belief — that every celebration deserves a cake that's as special as the moment itself. We started as a small home bakery in Colombo and have grown into one of Sri Lanka's most loved cake brands.

Every cake we create is handcrafted using fresh, quality ingredients. No shortcuts. No artificial flavors. Just pure, honest baking — the way it should be.
